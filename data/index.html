About SAI42

SAI42 is an innovative IoT solution that merges high-performance hardware with a user-friendly online platform.

Our Block System collects vital sensor data for each plant, analyzing it in real time to determine precise watering requirements. This ensures your plants receive exactly what they need for optimal growth.

Meanwhile, these data are securely transmitted to our Web Platform, enabling you to monitor and control your irrigation settings effortlessly from anywhere.
